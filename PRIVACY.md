# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

## Overview

Sequant is a local development workflow tool for Claude Code. It does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## Data Collection

Sequant collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No telemetry or analytics
- No personal information
- No usage tracking
- No network requests to Sequant servers

## How Sequant Works

All processing occurs locally on your machine. Sequant interacts only with:

- **Your local filesystem** — reading and writing project files
- **GitHub API** — via the `gh` CLI you have installed, using your existing authentication
- **npm registry** — only during installation or updates

These interactions use your own credentials and are initiated by you.

## Third-Party Services

Sequant does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at [github.com/sequant-io/sequant](https://github.com/sequant-io/sequant/issues).
